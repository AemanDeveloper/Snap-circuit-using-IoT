Alarm Light & Sound

The circuit alarm light and sound system is a safety device designed to alert users of potential electrical hazards or malfunctions within an electrical circuit. This innovative system combines a visual indicator, such as a bright LED light, and an audible alarm, typically a loud siren or beeping noise, to effectively capture the attention of nearby individuals

Flashing Lamp Circuit

The flashing light typically consists of a light-emitting diode (LED) or an incandescent bulb connected to a flashing circuit. The circuit is designed to periodically interrupt the power supply to the light, creating the flashing effect. The frequency and pattern of these flashes can be customized based on the specific application and requirements

Tilt Alarm Circuit

The Tilt Alarm Circuit is a security system designed to alert users when an object or surface experiences an unintended tilt or movement. This device can be used to monitor and protect valuable items, sensitive equipment, or areas where unauthorized access may be a concern. Vibration Switch Circuit

Designed to detect and respond to vibrations or mechanical shocks within an electrical circuit. It serves as a protective mechanism that activates or deactivates a system or component based on presence of vibrations
